Explore the Majesty of Gondar

Known as the "Camelot of Africa," Gondar's landscape is punctuated by imperial fortresses and churches. Just 10 km from Gondar Airport with complimentary shuttle, the hotel is minutes from Fasilida's Bath, the Royal Enclosure, and Debre Berhan Selassie Church.

Gondar Castle (Fasil Ghebbi)

Established in the 17th century by Emperor Fasilides, this fortress city served as the home of Ethiopia's emperors. Its unique architecture combines Indian, Arabic, and Baroque influences.

Simien Mountains

120 KM

An awe-inspiring landscape of jagged peaks and deep valleys, home to the rare Gelada baboon and Walia ibex.

Berhan Selassie

2.5 KM

Famous for its ceiling covered in the faces of angels, this 17th-century church is an Ethiopian artistic masterpiece.

Kuskuam Palaces

3.8 KM

The imperial residence built by Empress Mentewab, featuring a massive banquet hall and three stone churches.

Gorgora

65 KM

A peaceful lakeside town on Lake Tana, offering monastic ruins and a glimpse into early Jesuit influence in Ethiopia.
