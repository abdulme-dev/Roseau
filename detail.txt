Roseau Hotel & Spa
Overview

Roseau Hotel & Spa is a modern hotel located in Gondar, Ethiopia, designed for business travelers, tourists, families, and couples seeking comfortable accommodation with wellness facilities. The hotel combines modern amenities with Ethiopian hospitality and provides convenient access to Gondar's historic attractions, including the Royal Enclosure and Fasilida's Bath.
